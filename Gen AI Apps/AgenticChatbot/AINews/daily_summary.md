# Daily AI News Summary

### **2025-09-02**
- Reliance Industries launches a new AI-focused subsidiary to build gigawatt-scale data centers and expand partnerships with global tech giants. Reliance Industries, parent company of India's largest service provider, Reliance Jio, has announced the launch of a new subsidiary, Reliance Intelligence, focused on artificial intelligence (AI). Apart from setting up data centers, Reliance Intelligence will form global partnerships to "deliver performance leadership, resilient supply and India-first compliance for AI." It announced the expansion of its partnerships with tech giants, Google and Meta, for AI applications and services. Google and Meta hold 7.7% and 9.99% stakes in Jio Platforms, the parent company of Reliance Jio, India’s largest service provider. Reliance Industries also announced a joint venture with Mark Zuckerberg's Meta to develop Llama-based agentic enterprise AI platforms and tools.
